Fernando Muro

Welcome to my website! I'm a professor of mathematics at the University of Seville. Here, you'll mostly find information about my research.

Find Out More

Papers, preprints, slides...

I work on algebraic topology, specifically on homotopy theory, and I also interact with related fields such as category theory, homological algebra, K-theory, and representation theory.
